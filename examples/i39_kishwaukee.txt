3-span continuous steel plate girder bridge carrying I-39 over the Kishwaukee River
in Winnebago County, northern Illinois. Spans are 315-420-315 feet. 7 girder lines
at 9.5' spacing with a 40' deck width. Constant 168" depth, HPS 70W flanges with
50W webs (hybrid). 84" diameter columns on 96" drilled shafts. PTFE sliding bearings
on steel sole plates. Erected by incremental launch with an 80-ft king-post truss
launching nose. Existing 5-span PT segmental box girder must be demolished first.
