Cable-stayed bridge over the Ohio River connecting Kentucky and West Virginia.
Main span 800 feet with 400-foot back spans. Single plane of cables from a 350-foot
concrete A-frame tower. Composite steel edge girder deck system. Deep foundations
on drilled shafts through alluvial deposits to limestone. Navigable waterway with
vessel collision requirements. High seismic zone (near New Madrid). Cantilever erection
from tower with temporary stays.
